About

Welcome to Jhigu Chhen, a cozy spot in Lokanthali, Bhaktapur, where you can dive into the heart of Newari cuisine. Our place is all about celebrating Nepal's rich food culture with dishes passed down through generations. We're passionate about serving up the best of Newari flavors, from hearty kwati soup to spicy choila and sweet yomari dumplings. Step into our restaurant and you'll feel the warmth of our welcoming atmosphere, filled with traditional Newari decorations that tell stories of our heritage. Our team is here to make sure your dining experience is top-notch. Whether you're a foodie or just curious about trying something new, we're ready to guide you through our menu and make your visit memorable. Come visit us at Jhigu Chhen for a taste of Nepal's newari culinary tradition that'll leave you wanting more.
